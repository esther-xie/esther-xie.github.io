TRANSIENT SPACE

Academic Project

Fall 2021

GSD 6338 Introduction to Computational Design

Team member: Esther XIE, Angela ZHU, Gerardo CORONA

Role in Team: concept development (70%), programming (80%), representation (30%)

In this project, I cooperated with architects to assist with the campus masterplan, specifically traffic planning and deciding the elevation of each building. I was also responsible for the landscape design of the project, including the entry plaza, playground, ponds, and parking lots. I also participated in graphic representation and preparation for construction documentation. All rights reserved by L&M Design Lab .
