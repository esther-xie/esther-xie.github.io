ROME

Group Project

Spring 2018

Team Members: Lucas Bulger, Venus Dulani, Zachary Falk, Aubree Jones, Xuezhen (Esther) Xie

Rome Workshop, Cornell University

Instructors: Thomas J. Campanella and Greg Smith

Role in Team: field research, spatial analysis, graphic representation and writing

At the Rome Workshop, our team of five students analyzed historical and existing conditions of the Roman neighborhood of Torrevecchia and explored possible future trajectories of this dynamic area. With its vibrant mix of market activity, varied subsectors of food systems, and immigrant communities each actively redefining Torrevecchiaâ€™s sense of place, this modest area has changed from a satellite residential district into a developing and self-sustaining neighborhood with an identity all its own. A comprehensive report with analysis of context, neighborhood statistics, and physical characteristics, in addition to three posters of summary, was produced through collaboration over the semester.

The full report is available on issuu .
