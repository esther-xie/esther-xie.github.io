Esther Xie, or 谢雪桢 (Xie Xue Zhen), is a designer with a concurrent degree in landscape architecture and urban and regional studies from Cornell University.

Esther is an urban enthusiast. She is driven to identify social problems through spatial configurations and to navigate the messy entanglements in urban environments.
